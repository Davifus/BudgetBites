OUR PURPOSE

As technology advances further so do many other things like convenience. This means we have become overly reliant on services like Uber Eats or Doordash for our dietary needs and it hampers the development of our skills in cooking. Our purpose for this Cookbook is to educate the younger generation on the importance of cooking at home for health and monetary reasons.
